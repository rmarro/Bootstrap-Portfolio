About Me

I'm a full stack developer with a background in health research/education, violence prevention, and advocacy. After completing Northwestern University's intensive 6 month coding bootcamp and loving every minute of it, I realized I want to change my career to one in tech continue my career in confronting issues of social inequality, but start using tech to do it.

I've worked most with the MERN stack, but am a quick and driven learner always seeking to expand my skills. In addition to a knack for problem solving, logic, and sweating every little detail, I also bring excellent collaboration and communication skills from a decade of leadership experience in volunteer organizations and non-profits.
